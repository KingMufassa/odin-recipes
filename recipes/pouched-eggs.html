Pouched-Egg

Pouched-Egg with toast

Ingredients

  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the water
  2. Crack the egg into a small bowl and gently slip it into the water
  3. Turn off the heat, cover the pan, and let sit for 4 minutes
  4. Remove the eggs with slotted spoon
